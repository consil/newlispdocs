Function: prob-f
------------------------------------

#### syntax: (prob-f *num-f* *int-df1* *int-df2*)

Returns the probability of an observed *F* statistic in *num-f* with
*int-df1* and *int-df2* degrees of freedom to be equal or greater under
the null hypothesis.
