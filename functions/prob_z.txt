Function: prob-z
------------------------------------

#### syntax: (prob-z *num-z*)

Returns the probability of *num-z*, not to exceed the observed value
where *num-z* is a normal distributed value with a mean of `0.0` and a
standard deviation of `1.0`.

    (prob-z 0.0)  â†’ 0.5
